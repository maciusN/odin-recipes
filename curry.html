Thai Curry

Delicious and simple Red Thai Curry

This is a recipe for a really delicious and rather simple Thai Curry.

It's so easy to make. It's cheap and you're gonna love it! :)

  • 500g Pack Frozen Asia Veggie Mix
  • Red Onions
  • 4 Tea Spoons of Red Curry Paste
  • 200ml Coconut Milk
  • 500g Chicken Breast Fillet
  • 100g Roasted Peanuts
  1. Cut the onions and the chicken breast filet.
  2. Fry the onions for 5 minutes and then add the Chicken to it. Wait untill its gold-brown.
  3. Now add the asia veggie mix to it and let it fry for another 10-15 minutes
  4. Once you're done add 4 spoons of red curry paste to it and let it fry for another 3-5 minutes.
  5. Add the coconut milk and the peanuts to it.
  6. Let it cook for another 10 minutes.
  7. You're done. Enjoy your delicious Thai Curry! :)
